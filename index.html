Welcome

lobSTR is a tool for profiling Short Tandem Repeats (STRs) from high throughput sequencing data.

Download lobSTR

See the download page. The latest available version of lobSTR is v3.0.3.

About lobSTR

The lobSTR publication is available here:
Gymrek M, Golan D, Rosset S, & Erlich Y. lobSTR: A short tandem repeat profiler for personal genomes. Genome Research. 2012 April 22.
