About lobSTR

The lobSTR site is maintained by Melissa Gymrek (mgymrek AT mit DOT edu) and Thomas Willems (twillems AT mit DOT edu).

The lobSTR publication is available here:
Gymrek M, Golan D, Rosset S, & Erlich Y. lobSTR: A short tandem repeat profiler for personal genomes. Genome Research. 2012 April 22.
